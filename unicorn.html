Overview

Bob the Unicorn is a little 2-level game I developed for a school project. I really wanted to make a game from scratch; draw all the game assets and develop the game. So, when one of my classes required me to make a game, I thought it was the perfect opportunity to bring Bob the Unicorn to life!

My Role

This was an independent project, so I was in charge of everything. This included character and level design, character art, animation, and game implementation. I implemented all game mechanics, including but not limited to player and camera controllers, enemies, menus, powerups, and a health system.
